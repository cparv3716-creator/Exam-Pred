# ISI Content Vertical

This directory is the local-first content boundary for Statstrive's ISI exam family.

## Programs

- `msqe/`: active Phase 1 vertical.
- `mstat/`: reserved for a future MStat research and content layer.
- `mmath/`: reserved for a future MMath research and content layer.

## Source policy

Website question content must come from verified `.json`, `.md`, `.txt`, or `.tex` files. PDF files may be referenced as source documents but are not parsed directly by the website loader. Uncertain mathematics or incomplete extraction must remain marked for review.
